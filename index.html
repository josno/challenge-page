30 Projects In 30 Days Projects

A list of projects I created over 30 days.

I took this on as a personal challenge for myself to focus on being creative and continue to build. This challenge refocused my love for development and creation. Regardless of how well or badly some of these turned out I am proud that I stuck with the 30 days! A project a day was defined as a component, a page or something produced related to programming such as a pull request. I also gave myself more than one day to finish each project as long as I have 30 completed projects at the end of the challenge. You can checkout my writing here where I write about what I learned and how the projects will help me improve my skills.
